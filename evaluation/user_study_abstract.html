Image Tagging Instructions (Click to expand)

Determine if the sentences describe the clip art images.

In this study, you will be presented 5 groups of clip art images. Each group includes 4 images.

Each group of images is associated with 3 short sentences, as shown in the example below.

Your job is to help us determine if each sentence matches the content of the corresponding clip art. You can choose from 3 options:

"True" if the sentence matches the image, "False" if not, "Unknown" if you're not sure.

Introduction

Brief Introduction and Examples

A group of images typically depict a scene where children are playing. Each image is composed with the clip arts (or objects) shown below. When comparing the sentences and the images, please pay attention to

  1. if the objects mentioned in the sentence occur in the image,

  2. if the poses (e.g., arms up, kick), facial expressions (e.g. happy, sad) and accessaries (e.g. hat) of the children are matched,

  3. if the locations of the objects make sense.
